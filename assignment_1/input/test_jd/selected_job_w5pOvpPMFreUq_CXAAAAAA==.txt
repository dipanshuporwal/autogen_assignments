Job Summary:

2nd Order Solutions is a niche consulting firm focused on delivering data-driven solutions in the credit and risk space. As a full-time data scientist, you'll tackle real-world challenges for financial institutions and fintechs, transforming complex data into actionable insights using advanced analytics and machine learning.

Responsibilities:

• Play a key role on project teams that tackle real-world challenges for top-tier financial institutions and fast-growing fintechs.

• Transform messy, complex data into clear, actionable insights using a mix of advanced analytics, machine learning, and business strategy.

• Develop custom algorithms and build end-to-end machine learning pipelines.

• Help clients make more informed credit decisions and drive measurable results.

• Collaborate closely with other talented data scientists and business analysts, blending technical skills with creative problem-solving and business acumen.

• Learn how to communicate findings clearly and persuasively to both technical and non-technical audiences.

Qualifications:

Required:

• You’re on track to graduate in Fall 2025 or Spring/Summer 2026.

• You’re pursuing an undergraduate or graduate degree in a quantitative field such as data science, mathematics, statistics, engineering, or a related discipline.

• You have a strong foundation in statistical concepts and a working knowledge of machine learning techniques.

• You have experience working with real-world data, whether through coursework, research, or internships, and can extract meaningful insights from messy datasets.

• You enjoy solving business problems just as much as building models and always seek to understand the 'why' behind the data.

• You're proficient in Python (or R, though we prefer Python), and can efficiently code, analyze, and visualize data.

Preferred:

• Prior experience in fast-paced, analytics-driven environments, such as internships, research, or intensive coursework.

• Confidence in building models from the ground up, rather than relying solely on existing templates or notebooks.

• Strong communication skills, with the ability to translate technical findings into business-relevant insights for diverse audiences.

Company:

2nd Order Solutions is a consulting firm that specializes in credit strategies for small lending startups to top banks for its clients. Founded in 2008, the company is headquartered in Glen Allen, Virginia, USA, with a team of 11-50 employees. The company is currently Early Stage. 2nd Order Solutions has a track record of offering H1B sponsorships.
